Stone Gabions

A gabion is a container for rocks used where the flow of water would overtop or wash away other soil retention and water infiltration systems.

Stone Bunds

Stone bunds are structures constructed across a slope to slow the flow of water so that eroded soil particles carried in flowing water are deposited.

Vegetative Control Methods

Vegetation lines of grasses, shrubs or trees planted across the slope on the contour act as natural barriers to control erosion.

Dead Level Contours

Dead level contours are channels dug into the field with a zero gradient or slope, meaning water doesn't flow away but is instead harnessed and stored in the channel.

Half-moons

IHalf-moons, also known as semi-circular bunds are water-harvesting structures used to capture and retain rainfall on rangeland and in agricultural fields.

Infiltration Trenches

Infiltration trenches are a storm water management technique designed to capture sediment and infiltrate runoff water into the soil.

Climate Smart Agriculture

CSA is a strategy that encourages climate change-resistant, sustainable agricultural methods. .

Community Based Management

CBM is whereby the community is involved in identifying the problem, coming up with the answers, planning, implementing and conducting monitoring and evaluation on their own

Disaster Risk Reduction Committees

These are local groups tasked with planning and implementing strategies to reduce the risks associated with natural and man-made disasters.

Gender Responsive SLM

Gender-Responsive Sustainable Land Management (SLM) aims to address and overcome gender-related barriers that prevent women from adopting sustainable land management practices.

Payment for Ecosystem Services (PES)

PES is seen as a voluntary transaction in which users of ecosystem services make payments to providers, as long as they guarantee the flow of ecosystem services, for example, drinking water and climate stabilization..

Farmerled SLM Initiatives

These are projects and practices developed and implemented by farmers themselves to manage land sustainably
